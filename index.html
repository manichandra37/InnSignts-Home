Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Get answers to the most common questions about InnSights hotel management platform.

How does the competitor price tracking work?

+

Our platform automatically fetches and displays your competitors' prices in real-time, allowing you to compare rates across multiple dates and make informed pricing decisions to stay competitive.

Can I track prices for multiple dates at once?

+

Absolutely! Our matrix pricing feature allows you to view and compare hotel prices across multiple dates simultaneously, giving you a comprehensive view of market trends and competitor strategies.

How does the inventory management work?

+

Our inventory system tracks all your hotel supplies in real-time, manages purchase receipts digitally, and sends automated alerts when items are running low, ensuring you never run out of essential supplies.

What events can I track with the platform?

+

Our event intelligence feature tracks local festivals, conferences, concerts, sports events, and other activities that could impact hotel demand in your area, helping you adjust pricing strategies accordingly.

How does staff scheduling work?

+

Our staff scheduling tool allows you to create and manage employee schedules, track availability, assign shifts, and ensure optimal coverage for your hotel operations with an intuitive drag-and-drop interface.

Is there a mobile app available?

+

no not yet, but our platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops, allowing you to manage your hotel operations from anywhere, anytime.
